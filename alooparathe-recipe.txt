aloo paratha recipe is paratha stuffed with spiced aloo or potato mixture. the aloo or mixture can be made as per your taste and flavor. 
the stuffing which i am presenting here is very simple and easy to make. this aloo paratha recipe is my mom-in-law’s recipe. 
aloo parathas is a regular breakfast at our place. 
in punjab the parathas are made with lots of ghee and then served with dollops of white butter or makhan. white butter can be easily made at home.
